Coffee

Mocha

Espresso combined with chocolate syrup or chocolate powder, steamed milk, and topped with whipped cream and chocolate shavings.

Espresso

A concentrated coffee made by forcing hot water through finely ground coffee beans.

Macchiato

Espresso with a small amount of frothed milk on top.

Cold Brew

Coffee brewed with cold water over a long period, resulting in a smoother and less acidic flavour.

Flat White

Espresso with velvety steamed milk, creating a smooth and creamy texture.

Cappuccino

Equal parts of espresso, steamed milk, and frothed milk, often topped with a sprinkle of cocoa or cinnamon.

Drip Coffee

Regular brewed coffee made by pouring hot water over ground coffee beans.

Americano

A shot of espresso diluted with hot water.

Frappucino

A blended iced coffee or coffee-based beverage, often with added flavors, syrups, or toppings.

Latte

Espresso with steamed milk and a small layer of frothed milk on top.

Tea

Green Tea

Unoxidized tea leaves, known for their fresh and grassy flavor.

Herbal Tea

Infusions made from herbs, flowers, or fruits, often caffeine-free.

Jasmine Tea

Aroma of taste of jasmine are infused into the tea leaves, resulting in a fragrant and slightly sweet brew.

Iced Tea

Chilled tea served over ice, usually sweetened or flavored.

Chai Latte

Spiced tea blended with steamed milk, creating a fragrant and creamy beverage.

Pastry

Muffin

Individual-sized cakes with various flavors such as blueberry, chocolate chip, or bran.

Brownies

Rich and fudgy chocolate squares often topped with nuts or frosting.

Cookies

Sweet baked treats available in various flavors like chocolate chip, oatmeal raisin, or sugar cookies.

Custard Yogurt Toast

A delightful and simple dish that combines the creaminess of custard and yogurt with the crunchiness of toast.
