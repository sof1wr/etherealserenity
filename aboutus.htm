About Us

“Coffee: The sparks for conversations, creativity ignites, and revolutions launch.”

Kuala Lumpur City is about energy and creativity, and Ethereal Serenity exist to inspire and energize Kuala Lumpur citizens, We start with the best coffee and best baristas in the city, add amazing beautiful arts, and bring you the most fascinating publication.

Our obsession with great coffee comes from when we realized that the immersive culture around coffee in Malaysia has been increasing. Back in 2017, I had a vision of creating an environmentally conscious coffee shop and roastery that combined the craft coffee's art and precision with a warm and inclusive atmosphere .Our mission is to make this transition as honest as possible in our roasting, brewing, knowledge, actions, words, exclusivity, actions and expression. We prepare the coffee for you; you create the next chapter in the coffee’s history
